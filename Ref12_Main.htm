Hidden Games Room!

This is my off-the-record game collection: games that didn’t quite fit anywhere else. They’re not CRASH Smashes, they’re not by Ultimate, and they’re not titles I spent my youth glued to… but they’re far too good, or iconic to be left out in the cold.

Inside this hidden area you’ll find a small sample of my personal favourite commercial releases, homebrew gems, and other titles that may not necessarily be the best example of gaming code ever written, but are none-the-less an important part of Spectrum gaming history.
