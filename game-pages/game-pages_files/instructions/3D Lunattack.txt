ATTACK ON ATLANTIS
(Century City)


LOADING INSTRUCTIONS
To Load ---- Type LOAD "" (Press Enter)


OVERVIEW

Controls ... A = Down    M = Drop Bomb
             Q = Up      Sym/Shift = Fire Laser
             O = Slower  H = Stop On
             P = Faster  S = Stop Off

You have to battle your way past enemy ships, underwater torpedoes and
missiles, then dodge the indestructible fireballs and combat dangerous
mines.

Before you reach your objective and save Atlantis you have to combat
all the underwater evils on one fiery screen. Keep your fuel tanks
full by destroying the fuel dumps, and beware of crashing into under-
sea mountains, which can first be seen on your radar. Change the game
speed to suit your own skill level.
