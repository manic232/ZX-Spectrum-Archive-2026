OOZE

After years of experimentation at the hands of the machine your
containment unit has malfunctioned.

you are free.

Using the floors and ceilings explore the vast complex avoid toxic
slugs and gas, dodge lethal traps and robots and deactivate the
various force fields to find your way out.

Who knows what awaits you outside the walls of the high security
research complex compound.

In order to break free completely you must find and install the
four coloured code keys that will enable you to pass their respective
force fields and finally reach the exit.

With each code key found and installed you will be able to proceed
a little deeper into the facility in your search for the others.
Make sure that you install each code key once you find it as without
doing so you will not be able to proceed with your escape and will
be forever condemned to roam within the metal cage that is this
secret facility.

Control ooze using either the keyboard or joystick your control
method can be decided at the main menu reversing gravity will instantly
fit you from the game menu.

The controls are simple.

O = Left
P = Right
Space = Reverse gravity
M = Turn music on/off (at main menu screen)

Reversing gravity will instantly flip you from the floor to the
ceiling. This enables you to avoid the many automated patrol droids
and other hazards you will find in the facility, as well as allowing
you to navigate the entire area successfully.

Your escape was not anticipated so measures have not been taken
to secure the facility against such a situation. Use this to your
advantage if you cannot pass an area by the ground use the ceiling.
Ooze is weak to biological containments and you will die upon contact
with them or any of the electrical security devices. Be careful
with luck and skill you shall escape.
