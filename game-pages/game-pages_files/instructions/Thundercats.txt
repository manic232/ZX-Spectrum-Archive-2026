THUNDERCATS

During a raid by the wicked MOLEMEN, agents of the evil MUMM-RA 
entered the CATS-LAIR, kidnapped several members of the Thundercats 
team and stole the Eye of Thundera, the magical jewel which holds 
the mystical power of the sword of omens.  LION-O was out on a 
mission when the raid took ploace but vows that, as the eye was 
given into his safe-keeping, he must go to Castle Plundar, rescue 
his team-mates and retrieve the Eye.

Can LION-O battle his way past MUMM-RA's henchmenin the forests 
and underground caverns, and return the power of the sword of 
omens and glory to the Thundercats' name?

PLAYING INSTRUCTIONS

Spectrum:
Joystick or redefinable keyboard.

Extra weapons, bonuses and lives can be collected by hitting 
mushrooms and tress with your current weapon.  A bonus is 
awarded if the level is completed within the time limit.  
You also get a "kill" bonus according to the number of 
enemies destroyed.  On the special mission levels where you 
have to rescue a team-mate be careful as you only get one chance.  
If you get into a tight spot try jumping over the enemy and 
killing him from behind.  Good luck!
