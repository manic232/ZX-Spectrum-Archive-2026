Nohzdyve

 

Created By:  Matthew Westcott, Clayton McDermott, Brian Reitzell
Release Year: 2018

This is the game from Black Mirror: Bandersnatch.

You're falling fast through the sky! Collect eyeballs and avoid the buildings and other hazards. Perfection is key. This was truly a five star game by none other than Colin Ritman.

Play Nohzdyve on your ZX Spectrum emulator. Warning: You may become addicted to increasing your score.
