KOSMIC KANGA
(Micromania)
by Dominic Wood
Cover design by Mark Candlin


KOSMIC KANGA - a multi-screen arcade standard, original action game.
Featuring superbly animated graphics and packed full of arcade
features. Help Kanga find his spaceship so he can return home to his
planet. Take control of Kanga as he leaps about buildings, trees,
clouds, mountains, etc. Kanga scores points by picking up bonuses,
throwing boxing gloves at his enemies, and by completing each
different screen.

LOADING INSTRUCTIONS
1. Connect lead to EAR socket of Spectrum from EAR socket on recorder.
2. Rewind tape to beginning.
3. Set Volume Control as per Sinclair manual.
4. Type LOAD""
5. Press ENTER key.
6. Press PLAY on your cassette recorder.
7. KOSMIC KANGA will now load and auto-run.
If the program does not load first time, repeat instructions but try a
different volume setting.

PLAYING CONTROLS
To increase height of KANGA's jumps use key A, to decrease height use
key Z, to move right M, left N, to throw a boxing glove press the
remaining keys on the bottom row.

ALSO FOR USE WITH AGF, PROTEK, KEMPSTON AND INTERFACE II JOYSTICK
INTERFACES.
