Chuckie Egg

PUBLISHER
A&F Software Ltd

Year
1984

ARCADE STYLE GAME
This eggcellent game is guaranteed to be salmonella 
free on your machines. It will give hours of entertainment 
and no matter how many times you unwrap the cassette and 
sample the goodies it will neither make you fat nor rot your teeth!

Who'd have thought a country farmyard could be so stressful? 
You must collect the eggs before the nasties get out and eat 
up all your corn. Watch out for the crazy duck — if she gets 
out of the cage, you're in real trouble! You must collect 
all the eggs to proceed to the next screen.

CONTROLS
Select your own keys. Not compatible with Kempston joystick. 
Other joysticks may be used by selecting the redefine keys 
option and moving the joystick in the appropriate direction 
when asked. Full instructions included in the program.

PLAYERS
Up to 4 players can compete against one another.

LOADING
To load the program use: LOAD"" Should you have any problems 
loading the tape, rewind and adjust the volume/tone controls 
before trying again.

INSTRUCTIONS
The game plays over 4 groups each of 8 screens which become 
progressively more difficult. Survive all 32 screens and the 
speed then increases to present even greater challenges. 
The game will play an indefinite number of levels. There is 
no limit to the high score table!
