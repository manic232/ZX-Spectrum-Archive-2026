SCOOBY DOO & SCRAPPY DOO

Another wild and exciting adventure is about to begin for Scooby, Scrappy,
Shaggy and the rest of the gang. Shaggy has followed Scooby, who with his
everlasting urge for food has decided to wander off in search of a quick
snack.

After a time the gang notice that both Scooby and Shaggy are missing and
become deeply concerned. As quick as a flash, Scrappy Doo springs into
action volunteering to go and find them.

The search begins.....

CONTROLS & PLAYING THE GAME
Scrappy Doo can walk, jump left and jump right. He can also punch the nasties
and baddies, if the fire button is pressed for a longer period the he throws a
MEGA punch.

LOADING INSTRUCTIONS
48K					128K
Type LOAD "" and press ENTER		Press ENTER on loader

DEFAULT KEYS
Q	- Jump (or up if on hoverboard)
A	- Down (only if on hoverboard)
O	- Left
P	- Right
SPACE	- Punch (hold down longer for MEGA punch
F	- Pause (fire to restart)

Keys redefinable or use Sinclair or Kempston compatible joystick.
