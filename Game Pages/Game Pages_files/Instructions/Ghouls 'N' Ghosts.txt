INSTRUCTIONS
Arthur is back! Two years after rescuing his dusky eyed maiden
from the demon king, he must save her once more from him. 

Special weapons: When the player finds a special weapon(these are
found in  chests) this can be activated by holding down the fire
button/key until the indicator  on the status panel lights up
fully, and then releasing the fire button/key. 
 
Continue option: 
When the player has lost all of his lives, he will be given the
option to continue from where he last left off, this option will
be available for a limited number of times(the number of
'continues' will vary from format to format)
