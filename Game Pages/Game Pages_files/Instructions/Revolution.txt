REVOLUTION
Vortex Software

---------
OBJECTIVE
---------
To reach Level 8 and beyond. Once elevated you must solve the 
four puzzles on each level and return successfully to the 
elevator within the time limit displayed. You will then be
transported to the next level and awarded an extra life (max. 
of 5 lives).

----------------
SOLVING A PUZZLE
----------------
Within each puzzle are two grey blocks of matter. Touch one and
it becomes unstable for a short period of time. If within that
time the second block is touched, then both blocks will 
vapourise harmlessly.

There are several ways to solve each puzzle. However, as you
progress through the stack of levels the unstable period 
becomes shorter. The solutions therefore become fewer and fewer.

--------------
THE MAP SCREEN
--------------
This displays the following:

1. Level of achievement.
2. Number of lives remaining.
3. Location of puzzles yet to be solved.
4. Current location.

------------
BALL CONTROL
------------
1. Directional control.

The direction of the ball cannot be changed in mid-flight. It 
can only be altered on contact with the ground or other objects.

2. Bounce energy.

The top half of the on screen meter indicates the present bounce
energy of the ball. This determines the height at which the ball
can bounce.

The lower half of the on screen meter indicates the desired 
energy setting. This can be changed by holding down the fire
button or appropiate key until the required energy setting is
reached. Release to activate the new setting.

----------------
KEYBOARD CONTROL
----------------
Q = Forward
A = Back
K = Left
L = Right

Use combinations of above keys for diagonal movement.

Z to N or space = Bounce energy
M = Map, enter to return to game
P = Pause, enter to return to game
A + G = Abort game

------------------------------
JOYSTICK CONTROL (RECOMMENDED)
------------------------------
Use the Joystick for directional control. The fire button sets 
the bounce energy.

For other functions use keyboard.
