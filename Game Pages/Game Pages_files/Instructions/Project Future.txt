PROJECT FUTURE Loading instructions
1 Connect lead to EAR socket of Spectrum from EAR socket on
     the tape recorder.
2 Rewind tape to the beginning.
3 Set Volume Control as per Sinclair manual
4 Type LOAD"" and press ENTER key
5 Press PLAY on your tape recorder
6 PROJECT FUTURE will now LOAD and AUTO RUN.

If the program does not load first time, repeat instructions
but try a different volume setting.

PROJECT FUTURE Playing Instructions

The SS FUTURE is a vast Space Ship, spread over 5 different
decks. Hidden in the laborinth are 8 destruct codes which
must be collected before the SELF DESTRUCT SYSTEM can be
activated. Once the S.D.S. of SS FUTURE has been activated
you must escape before detonation.

FACTS
Don't try using the Kempston option - the programmers have
got it all wrong and the up/down directions are reversed.

NOTES
Despite the similarity to Sabre Wulf, there were a lot of
other games that attempted to emulate the most successful
Ultimate creations, and Project Future is a nice game in its
own right, with lovely graphics. Look out for the natty
invincibility suit, and the jet-powered C5!
