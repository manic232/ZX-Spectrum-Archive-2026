Moon Cresta
-----------

Select game for 1 or 2 players. Use joystick or keyboard to move Rocket
Ships (1-3). Shoot and avoid the onslaught of attacking Kamikaze Aliens but
beware as their tactics and formulations will change as your skill
increases. Bonus is given at 30,000 points. Gams is over when all Rocket
Ships are destroyed.


BONUS DOCKING

Use Rocket controls to make a docking-pass fire for Retrofiring. The quicker
the docking the higher the Bonus. When docking is complete-greater fire
power is achieved. Name registration for best 5 scores.

ENJOY THE TRIP!

DOCKING

Points vary. The quicker the docking the higher the score.


CONTROL OPTIONS

Moon Cresta can be played via the keyboard (option 3) or with a joystick
(option 4).

Keyboard Control:
Keys of the players choice may be selected - see option 5. (When loaded
keys = Caps shift - Left, Z - Right and Space - Fire)

Joystick Control:
Select option 4 to select Kempston Joystick control. For other Joysticks
select Keyboard control and define keys (option 5) as shown below.

Sinclair Define Left = 6, Right = 7 & Fire = 0
AGF Define Left = 5, Right = 8 & Fire = 0
Protek Define Left = 5, Right = 8 & Fire = 0
