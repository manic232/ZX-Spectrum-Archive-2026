Land of Mire Mare

 

Created By: Luca Bordoni, Jarrod Bentley, Mister Beep, Chris Cowley
Release Year: 2014

This game has to be considered as a tribute, it's inspired by the unreleased Mire Mare game & characters created by Ultimated Play the Game in golden 80's.

In the Land of Mine Mare three volcanoes are on the brink to burn the whole territory. In order to avoid a disaster, Sabreman will have to find and use three magic jewels.
