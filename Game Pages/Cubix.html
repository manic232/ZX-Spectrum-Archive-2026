Cubix

 

Created By: Sergei Andreevich, Art-Top, UriS, Eugene Rogulin
Release Year: 2025 

A puzzle platformer with elements of metroidvania. The game has 6 large locations ("towers"), unlike each other, more than 10 various game mechanics, cute characters and mind-blowing logical puzzles. As the game progresses, the main character gains more and more abilities, allowing him to pass the "towers" non-linearly (i.e. in different ways and routes), the mechanics of the towers change, and the complexity of the puzzles increases.
