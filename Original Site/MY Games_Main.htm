My Games

There is not a lot to this section I'm afraid, merely Instructions, a number of reviews from the pages of CRASH and a few words from me on the games I can recall owning as a child.

I only ever owned a 48K Spectrum so you wont find and 128 titles here, but to be honest the 128K games were just the same as the 48K ones, just with better sound (In my opinion).

I have attempted to bring together all the games I can remember having stored on top of my toy box lid in my bedroom.  I'm not saying they are all here, half the games I played I can not even remember the name of, but the biggies are here, Manic Miner, Jet-Set Willy etc... Enjoy (I did!!)

The list of games to the left link to instructions and information for the games in the Games - My Games folder.  Most are duplicates form other areas, because as I'm sure you can work out, I owned some titles that were CRASH Smashes, Ultimate or just ones in Games - Archive.

As with CRASH Smashes, entries that are Greyed-Out mean that the instructions are not available for that game.

Don't forget about the good old Extras folder for cover images, ads and other goodies.

My Memories

I can see myself now, sitting at my desk (well a chest of draws actually) happily waiting 3 or 4 minuets for a game to load, excitedly anticipating what pleasures the game would bring, only for the bloody thing to crash and have to start all over again.  But that's the way it was then, we all accepted it because we didn't know any different.

When you consider the limited technical ability of the Spectrum, the excitement it created for me (and millions of others) was surprising, but you have to remember there was little else around at the time.  I can remember my Grandmother (Muzz) coming up to my room sometimes after I told her of my latest Spectrum game purchase, she would sit there for ages looking at the game as I excitedly showed her the wonders it had to offer.  She of course was not in the slightest bit interested, but pretended for me!!

The games I remember playing most were Jet Set Willy, Manic Miner, Splat, Alien 8, Knight Lore, Underwurlde, Wheelie and 3D Death Chase.  there were many others of course, but for some reason these stick in my mind most.
