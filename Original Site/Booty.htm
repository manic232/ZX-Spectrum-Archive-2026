Booty

 

 

PUBLISHER

Firebird Software

 

AUTHOR

John F Cain

 

YEAR

1984

 

DESCRIPTION

Platform game where a certain amount of Booty must be collected from the ship.  This involves collecting keys to open locked doors.

 

CONTROLS

Keyboard controls are re-definable / Joysticks

 

INSTRUCTIONS

Jim the Cabin Boy has to collect all the items of Booty from the ship by collecting keys that open locked doors. Hazards include the pirates who patrol the rooms, rats which crawl along the deck and the ships parrot which fly's around the ship. Also some booty is booby trapped and explodes when Jim try's to collect it. Once all the booty is collected, Jim has 45 seconds to find the key to the next pile of booty!

 

------------------------------------

 

SEQUELS

Moonlight Madness use a virtually identical game engine, although story-wise it is completely different.

 

SCORES RECEIVED

93% - Crash 10 (October 1984)

 

NOTES

Classic Game, just as fun today.  One annoying bug occurs when Jim falls of a ledge and the game can sometime freeze up.

 

CHEATS

Here's how to access the hidden game: At the Booty title page, enter the pokes: 52796,32 and 52798,58.  Then select your controls and start the game as normal.
