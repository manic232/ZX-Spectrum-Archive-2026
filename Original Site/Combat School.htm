Combat School

 

 

PUBLISHER

Ocean 1987


CONTROLS

Keyboard, Cursor, Kempston

AIM

You are a raw recruit entering the US Marines' famed Combat School. Can you survive the gruelling tests of endurance and enter the Marine Corps?

GAMEPLAY

Combat School is rather like a military version of Daley Thompson's Decathlon! There are seven different tests for you to undergo:
 
1. ASSAULT COURSE - walls of different heights and an "arm walk". A meter at the bottom of the screen shows the muscular power you're generating.
2. FIRING RANGE - hit at least 38 targets to qualify.
3. "IRON MAN" RACE - run through rough country strewn with boulders, water hazards and bridges; then swim across a fast-flowing river full of logs.
4. FIRING RANGE - hit 95 robot tanks to qualify.
5. ARM WRESTLING - a man-to-man contest of bicep supremacy.
6. FIRING RANGE - hit lots of targets and avoid hitting red ones.
7. UNARMED COMBAT - take on your instructor!


Failing any of these events (apart from number 5) means you have to do a physical penance - which means chin-ups! Once you have completed all the events, you must attempt a full mission, to rescue a hostage from an American embassy.
