The Wild Bunch

 

 

PUBLISHER
Firebird

AUTHOR
Kevin Smith

YEAR
1984

DESCRIPTION
Menu-based text adventure based on the Wild West

CONTROLS
Simply input options as prompted on the main screen

INSTRUCTIONS
A man has been shot and you were discovered on the scene.  You manage to take a note of the description of the real killer, and make a getaway before being arrested.  But the sheriff has put a Pinkerton Agent on your trail, so you must find and arrest the real killer before the agent arrests you.  You must keep your strength up by buying equipment and drinking in the tavern.

 

--------------------------------

CHEATS
With the use of snapshotting available on the emulators, it is easy enough to challenge everybody, and if they turn out to be innocent, simply reload the saved snapshot. If the are one of the wild Bunch, shoot `em!!

NOTES
Original menu based adventure which although it can be repetitive, is still quite enjoyable today.
