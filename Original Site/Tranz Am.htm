Tranz Am

 

 

Publisher: Ultimate Play The Game

 

Authors: Unknown

 

Year of Release: 1983

 

Description: The frantic chasing around a large playfield and the furious pursuit by the Black Turbos were classic gameplay elements. Trans Am arrived without too much pomp and ceremony in 1983 and, along with fellow early titles Jetpac, Pssst and Cookie, set the tone for what was to come.

 

Overview: Collect the 8 cups hidden throught the land, before your fuel runs out.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Controls:

Keyboard Controls:

Turn Anticlockwise using the following keys, Z,C,B,M.
Turn Clockwise using the following keys, X,V,N,SYMBOL SHIFT
Brake using any key on the second row, e.g. A,S,D,F, etc.
Accelerator using any key on the third row, e.g. Q,W,E,R, etc.

 

Joystick Controls:

TRANS AM can be controlled using Kempston Competition-Pro joysticks.

 

Inlay Card Text:

The year is 3472 and all that remains on Earth are the remnants of a once  great continent, a barren land, a desolate place of sand, rocks and boulders.

But...
life adapts quickly and brings with it a New Age, a time ruled by cars and  trophies, a land where petrol replaces gold and esteem is possession of the  8 Great Cups of Ultimate.

Possessed for too long by the 'Deadly Black Turbos', the 8 cups remain hidden throughout the land, their strange inscriptions long since forgotten and  their locations reverently protected.

Your chance...

A Super Blown Red Racer equipped with technology far advanced. A last chance  with a weapon, whose power far surpasses any Black Turbo.

One press and the massive engine thunders into life, the shrill rumble from  the exhausts and the hypnotic whirr beckon the Deadly Black Turbos. The noise  reaches crescendo and in one sudden lurch the Red Racer spits forward across  the land at incredible speed, in the last great battle of your life.

Use your skill to outwit and crash the Deadly Black Turbos and the instruments  to locate and collect the 8 Great Cups of Ultimate before you run out of fuel.
