There are a few Usenet newsgroups which are relevant to the good old Speccy; if you don't know much about Usenet, please read the group news.announce.newusers first!

  • comp.sys.sinclair is the main group, especially for the English speakers amongst you; if you don't have Usenet access, there is a mailing list mirror: send an email to lserv@psg.com with the following (and nothing else) in the body of the message:

    subscribe sincnews yourusername@your.domain

    [Can anyone confirm if this still exists? - Phil]

  • For the Spanish, es.comp.emuladors and es.comp.sistemas.sinclair both carry Sinclair-related traffic.
  • Whilst the Russians have fido7.zx-spectrum and relcom.comp.speccy.

  • For distributing binary files, the alt.binaries.comp.sinclair group is available; this does suffer the usual alt.binaries lack of distribution, lack of archiving and short retention time problems, so is by no means a permanent archive. Do read the alt.binaries.comp.sinclair FAQ before posting.

There are also a couple of Spectrum-related mailing lists about:

  • For TS2068 users: send a blank, subjectless e-mail to 2068-request@unixville.com for details of how to subscribe.
  • One about Your Sinclair and Amiga Power, hosted by E-Groups.
