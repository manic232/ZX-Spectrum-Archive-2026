The most active IRC channel these days appears to be '#speccy' on the Astrolink network. Appropriate servers are:

  • toronto.on.ca.astrolink.org (also known as 'studley.uk.eu.astrolink.org' for some reason).
  • london.uk.eu.astrolink.org
  • columbus.oh.us.astrolink.org
  • mexicocity.mx.astrolink.org
  • irc.astrolink.org

To join, you'll need the key, which is '16384'.

Other channels which may be worth checking out are '#z80' (available on the IRCnet network) and '#z80.rus' (accessible via 'irc.funet.hi' if not elsewhere).
