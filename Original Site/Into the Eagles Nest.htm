Into the Eagles Nest

 

 

PUBLISHER
Pandora

AUTHORS
Kevin Parker & Robin Chapman

YEAR
1986.

DESCRIPTION
A game of the Gauntlet genre. Set in a German Castle, you are an English soldier, set to rescue 3 prisoners held there.

<From back Sleeve>
You were briefed at eleven.  They told you you're the best.  You handle the worst jobs, the delicate jobs - the messy jobs no-one else will risk taking.  That's why you're here.  Your mission - to infiltrate and destroy the stronghold of a top enemy commander which serves as temporary barracks and armoury for the divisions under him - to rescue three fellow saboteurs held prisoner in the bowels of the fortress - to steal back as many antiquities as possible from the commander's private collection. So you're the best.  Prove it.

CONTROLS
Kempston, Sinclair or Cursor Joysticks
Keyboard  - Fully Redefinable

INSTRUCTIONS
Brief:
Six key German divisions are in your area - the main force is believed to be quartered in the fortress codenamed "Eagle's Nest". Three saboteurs sent to destroy the Eagle's Nest have been captured. The third managed to set up a network of explosives at key points throughout the fortress, but was captured before they could be detonated. It is up to you to rescue them and destroy the Eagle's Nest...

Objectives:
Infiltrate the fortress and rescue the three captives before they break under torture.  It is your aim to cause maximum havoc and confusion before finding and detonating the hidden explosive on all eight floors.  Whilst doing so it is in your interests to recover as many art treasures and jewels as possible from the commander's private collection.

 

-----------------------------------

NOTES
An enjoyable blast with surprisingly good graphics for a Spectrum.  There are enhanced effects in 128k mode (Music, etc.)

 

CHEATS
Enter the following names onto the scoreboard for good stuff:
DAS MAP - Map Mode
DAS CHT - Cheat Mode / Infy Lives
DAS NME - No Enemies
MAP OFF - Turn off Map Mode
