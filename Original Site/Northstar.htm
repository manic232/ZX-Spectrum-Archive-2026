North Star

 

 

PUBLISHER
Gremlin Graphics

AUTHOR
Jon O'Brien

YEAR
1988

DESCRIPTION
Right-scrolling Exolon-type game with nice colour graphics and animation.  Ten levels.

CONTROLS
Redefinable keyboard, Sinclair, Cursor or Kempston joysticks.

INSTRUCTIONS
Go right!

 

------------------------------

CHEATS
POKE 48371,180 gives you infinite lives, POKE 44433,0 removes all nasties.
