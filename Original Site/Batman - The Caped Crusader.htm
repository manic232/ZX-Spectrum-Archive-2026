INSTRUCTIONS
There are two separate "plots" revolving around Batman's deadliest enemies - The Penguin and The Joker:

1) A Bird In The Hand
The Penguin, having been released from jail, decides to set up an umbrella factory in Gotham City, but (of course) is secretly using it as a cover for his plan to take over the world with an army of robot penguins! As Batman, can you shut down the factory computer and prevent the world from getting the bird?

2) A Fete Worse Than Death
Robin has mysteriously disappeared, the only clue being a single  playing card left behind by his kidnapper - the Joker! On the flip side of the card is a message: "Robin heads for a fete worse than death, the fuse is lit - so don't waste your time, follow your nose and remember, there's no place like home! Boom! Boom!". Can you unravel the mystery, save Robin and defeat the Joker?

The presentation on screen simulates a comic strip with Gotham City and its inhabitants appearing in multiple overlaid panels. Any text (clues, descriptions etc.) is displayed in caption boxes in the appropriate panel. The player, as the Batman, has complete freedom to move around and interact with the numerous locations, solving puzzles en route.

By pressing down and fire together, Batman will enter the utility screen. This displays all the objects which you have picked up and also the control icons which are used to manipulate them. (You can also change various aspects of the game's displays and sound effects at this point). Batman's energy status bars and the game completion percentage are also displayed on this screen. By moving the Bat-cursor onto the object and pressing fire, then pressing the Utilise icon in the same manner, that object will be used.
