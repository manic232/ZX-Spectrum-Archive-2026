Death Star Interceptor

 

 

The evil Garth Fader has sent the most deadly weapons system in the Universe to finally destoy the last outpost of humanity on an insignificant little planet at the edge of the galaxy, called Earth. Ravaged by the many long decades of war the only form of defence left to protect the Earth is a squadron of three ion-drive Ford Prefects (2174 model) armed with laser cannon. Even worse you are the only pilot available! The Earthfederation has rapidily fitted these ships with remote control equipment so that you can control them in space. You must carefully pilot ships along a corridor on the surface of the Death Star and destroy the enemies squadrons of Quasar star fighters armed with the deadly and unstoppable plasma projectors. The sides and floor of the corridors are also protected by plasma enegy fields. If you come intom contact with plasma bolt or plasma field, an irreversible chain reaction will occur causing the molecular unbinding of the atoms of you ship which will disintegrate and slowly fade away before your eyes. Oh no! They are here already you are our only hope - you climb into the remote control capsule and your mission begins.
