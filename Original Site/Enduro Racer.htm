Enduro Racer

 

 

PUBLISHER

Activision

 

YEAR

1987

CONTROLS

Keyboard, Cursor, Kempston

AIM

Win a cross-country motorbike race.

GAMEPLAY

At the start of every stage a timer is set to 60 seconds, the limit within which the course must be completed. The other riders and the various obstacles on the road can slow you down or even throw you off your bike. There are five stages:

1. COUNTRY ROAD
2. DESERT
3. SWAMP CAUSEWAY
4. ARTIC
5. SEASIDE

 

-------------------------------------

COMMENTS

"A risky Spectrum conversion that has paid off handsomely, providing all the thrills and spills of the original."


RATING

92% (CRASH #40, May 1987)

NOW

Quite an enjoyable racing game, even if the graphics are a bit crude at times; the original is still in many arcades, which perhaps proves its lastability.
