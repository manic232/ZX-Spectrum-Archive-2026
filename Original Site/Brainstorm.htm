Brainstorm

 

 

LOADING
1. Connect the EAR plug on the computer to the EAR plug on the cassette playeR Disconnect the MIC lead.

2. Place the cassette in the player and rewind.

3. Type in LOAD"" then press ENTER.

4. BRAINSTORM will now automatically load. Several seconds later the BRAINSTORM loading screen will appear. If it does not load successfully, adjust the volume control on your cassette player and repeat the instructions. For complete loading instructions consult your Spectrum manual.

PLAY
One day Robin Banks was travelling the universe zapping aliens and generally minding his own business, when he was sucked into Professor Brainstorm's Warp Tunnel. Now he finds himself inside the Professor's castle surrounded by hungry Clones. Escape from the castle is his only hope of survival. Special weapons must be found to combat the dreaded Guardians of The Tower before escape is even possible.

JOYSTICK
Either Kempston, Sinclair 2 or Cursor joysticks may be used. Pull the joystick down to pick up/drop objects.

KEYBOARD
Thrust - Q

Left - 0

Right - P

Pick up/drop - A
Laser - Any of the bottom row keys.

REPAIRING DAMAGE
Press keys 1 - 4 or H - L

PAUSE
To pause the game press the 'ENTER' key, press it again to restart.

ABORT GAME
To abort the game you are playing first press the 'ENTER' key then the 'BREAK' key. This will return you to the option screen.
