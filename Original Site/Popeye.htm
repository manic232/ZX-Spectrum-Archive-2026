SOLUTION
Intro story
Olive Oil stands at her window waiting for her man to bring her hearts. Meanwhile the massive frame of Bluto thunders across the screen as he lies in wait for Popeye. The essence of game is to find all the hearts and return them to Olive before her lovemeter runs out. He must also collect spinach cans, keys and an assortment of objects to aid him during his journey. Spinach is the only way to revive Popeye following a blow from Bluto, vultures, witches on broomsticks and so on. Keys are needed to gain access to the chambers and rooms where more objects and obstacles lie. To reach the various objects Popeye can jump, climb up and down ropes and hitch UFO.


At first, excellent graphics (with the largest movable figures up to that time) will animate you. Consequence of this is Popeye slowness in response to your commands, but once when you have adjusted to the slightly slow reactions and find the way to avoid Bluto, you can really start getting into the game.

Step-by-step solution
First, you have to understand 'realisation of 3rd dimension' in this game, every screen has 3 or 4 layers. As a result, up and down keys are used in 2 ways, if you are climbing- it's ordinary up/down but while you walks- they change Popeye's depth position on the screen i.e. move him toward you or backwards. Furthermore, when Popeye have an object/heart above his head pressing up/down will result with a very nice jump.

The stars in the solution means that you've entered a new screen. If that's not so, you've made a mistake in your quest to complete Popeye.

General hints, special-tips
1- Don't give the hearts to Olive until the love meter gets low.


2- 'Green shot timing': Climb the rope until just Popeye's legs are in view, wait for red shot, then quickly climb up, go left and drop down onto cannon.


3- Walk screen-ward behind the stairway, then right to pick up that heart.


4- If you use save/load snap option on emulator, avoid spinach, you'll have bigger "pocket".
