3D CONSTRUCTION KIT

20. HINTS AND TIPS

  1. Save regularly.
  2. Have blank formatted discs or blank tapes ready for saving data.
  3. Always mention the Construction Kit release number and registration number in any correspondance.
  4. Colour sides of objects that can never be seen to invisible to increase performance.
  5. Care should be taken when entering Conditions as an infinate loop could be created effectively
    causing a crash. If in doubt save your data before testing a procedure you are unsure of.
