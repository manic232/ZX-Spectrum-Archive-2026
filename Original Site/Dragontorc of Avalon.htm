Dragontorc

Just cheats, no instructions

 

 

PUBLISHER
Hewson

YEAR
1986

DESCRIPTION
Dragonotorc is an Arcade Adeventure.

CHEATS
Wispwood Forest / Wierdhenge
Use the Bane spell to kill the wisps.


Trade the bow and the jewel with the elves to get a sickle and a message.


Use the sickle to cut the Energize plant.


Open the 2 slabs with the (m) symbol.  Then drop all and go down to the Vaults.


Kill the skeletons with missiles.


There are 4 chests (one is hidden under a toadstool): one contains a key which unlocks another.The second key unlocks the 2 remaining chests.  Thus you get the I rune, the Leyrod and the Halfmoon.


Fill the chalice with water, empty it in the basin : you get the Heal spell and the basin energizes you.


Find the X and T runes then use the runes on the E to make an EXIT.

Keep the (m) and the Halfmoon

At Wierdhenge (and at all stone circles), use Leyrod to reveal portals.


Here is the list of the portals.

Wierdhenge      --> Stoneways , Dragons Teeth
Stoneways       --> 7 Sisters , Trollstones , Torhenge
7 sisters       --> Wyrmcrag
Wyrmcrag        --> Trollstones
Torhenge        --> Wierdhenge
Dragon's Tooth  --> Torhenge , Dreamdowns
Dreamdowns      --> Wieldhenge , Trollstones


Webwood / 7 sisters
Kill the spiders with missiles.


Use (m) on the slab to open Merlyn's cave.  Use it to open chests in the cave.


Get the Warrior spell and the milk, reveal the portal under the table and exit.


Get the stick, use it to lever rocks and get the axe.

 

Use the milk on the hollow log to get Spike.  Axe the stump and use Spike to uncover the key.
In Merlyn's cave, unlock the door and get the (H)

Keep the (H) and Halfmoon.


Witchwoods / Stoneways
Use the bat, herb and frog on the cauldron to reveal the wand.The wand will open the 2 slabs and tame the Sword of Antimagic.


Exchange the jewel for the other halfmoon and complete the Moon.


Cut the broom with the Sword and use it to sweep leaves.Use the Moon on the rock to make a portal.

Keep the Moon , Sword and (H)


Sanctuary
If you walk on the cursed scroll, you'll be teleported into an empty room.


Use Leyrod to exit (it raises your score !)
Kill the goblins and demon with missiles and get the red key, Sunkey and Locate.


Use the Sword to uncurse the scroll.


Don't kill the spiders with missiles!  Instead, use the Sword to get spells.


In the dark room, use the Sword to get the wand.  Go to the dark room with a coffin.

 

Get the spell from the coffin (and cast Bane to kill the wraith).


Use the wand on the left wall to get a key.  It unlocks one of the 2 locked doors, and gives access to a chest and to the other key.


In the room with 2 doors side by side, go through the top door, use Locate to find a chest and get the Cursed key.  In the other room, use the Moon and the Sunkey to exit.

Keep the (H) , Sunkey and Cursed key.
       


Back to the Vaults of Locris
Unlock the door with the Sunkey, get the Undo spell.Kill the wraith and cast Undo on the doorway.  Cast Undo to get the first Dragontorc Crown.

Keep the (H), Cursed key and Crown.


Wolfwood / Dragon's Tooth
Kill the werewolves with the Warrior.


Get the Rod from the skeleton and use it on the leaves and pools.


Get the Crystal of Antithought.


Use the (H) to open the way to Halgor's Sanctuary.  Use (H) on the spiders (it's a good idea to cast slow before doing that) and get the spells.


Cast Return, get the key and open the 2 locked doors.  Use the Crown on the anvil.  Drop the (H) and Crystal.

Keep only the Cursed Key


Greatwood / Torhenge
Get a rabbit.Go to the room with 2 holes and put the rabbit in the left hole.

 

Get the key.Go kill the goblins and demon and get the ankh from the chest.


Put a rabbit on the carrots and open the slab with the ankh.Go down.  In the cursed crypt, go left and get the 2 other magic
symbols.  Use the Crown symbol to activate the exit portal.


Use the Cursed key to unlock doors, and get the chest key.  (Cast slow to help avoid the snakes).

 

Unlock the chest and get the door key.


Cast Bane to kill the wraiths.


In the last room, use the crown symbol on the coffin to get the Dragontorc Crown.

                * DON'T KILL THE HAND !!!!!! *

 Keep the Dragontorc Crown


Timberdown Forest / Dreamdowns
You can kill the axemen with missiles, but it's not necessary: the elf will kill them himself so just make sure he follows you.


Trade the bow for a nut and use the squirrel to crack the nut.


Use it to search the pile of leaves, too.


Light the torch.


Go to the ruins; get the snake symbol from the chest and use it to open slabs.

 

Get the Demon spell.


Go down to the ruins with the torch and the snake.  Burn the snakes and the generator.

Unlock the doors and the chest with the snake symbol and get the crown symbol.

 

Use it on the coffin to get the 3rd Dragontorc Crown.


Use the snake symbol to activate the portal.


Blasted Forest / Trollstones
Get the coin and throw it in the pool.  Go down.


Get the axe and use it to break rocks.  Get the symbol from the chest.


Use it to uncurse the door key and to open the exit portal.


Go to the demon room and cast Demon (set a thief...): your demon will steal the Dragontorc Crown for you.

Cast Return, use on your Crowns on the anvil then use the Crystal of Antithought.

Go back to the Cursed Crypt and tame the hand with the crystal.


Wyrmwood / Wyrmcrag
Get the 2 jewels and the bow and trade with the elves.


Kill snakes with lit torch and spiders with missiles Keep the big key, the torch and the hand and enter the citadel.


Open the doors near the entrance with the big key then drop it.


Use detect to reveal invisible monsters.


You'll find 2 chest keys; each opens the chest of the same color.


One of the 2 keys is cursed and creates snakes once you've moved it: use the Undo spell to cleanse it.


In one of the chests you'll find Morag's throne room key.

 

In the throne room, use the hand to steal the crown.


Go to the leftmost room and use the wand on the place where clouds are created.

 

You'll find a door key which opens the room to the lower right of the throne room.

 

There you'll find a chest: open it and uncurse it with the wand.  Get the key to the upper right room.

 

In the empty room, use the wand to reveal a chest, open the chest and uncurse it with the wand.

 

Get Mindkey.

 

Go to the room with a pentacle on the floor.

 

There is a locked door nearby.


Open it with mindkey.

 

Unlock the chest and uncurse it with the wand.


Now you should have 3 pentacles: one is red, the 2nd yellow and the 3rd white.

 

Use them in that order on the floor pentacle.

Keep the Dragon Crown


Cairndoom
Mindkey all the doors, missile the monsters.

 

Use Mindkey on the rock to free Merlyn and give him the Crown.
